Features
Everything Creators Need. Nothing They Don't.
Version 2 is here — now with Apple Log, time-lapse, customizable bitrate, and more.
🎬

Dual-Camera Recording

Record portrait (9:16) and landscape (16:9) video simultaneously using your iPhone's wide and ultra-wide lenses. Two synced files from one take.

📷

Single Lens Mode

Switch to single lens mode with front and back camera support. One camera, two perfectly cropped outputs.

⚡

4K & 1080p at 24/30/60fps

Full resolution control. Choose 4K or 1080p, pick your frame rate, and select MOV or MP4 format.

⏰

Time-Lapse Recording

NEW in v2 — Capture time-lapse videos in both dual lens and single lens mode. Both portrait and landscape files are saved.

🎯

Tap-to-Focus & AE/AF Lock

NEW in v2 — Tap anywhere to focus and lock exposure. A visual indicator confirms the lock. Tap again to unlock.

🔍

Pinch-to-Zoom

NEW in v2 — Smoothly zoom the back camera with a pinch gesture, restricted to wide lens range for stability.

⚙

Bitrate Quality Control

NEW in v2 — Choose Low, Medium, or High bitrate to balance file size versus quality for your workflow.

⏸

Pause & Resume

NEW in v2 — Pause your recording and resume seamlessly. Audio and video stay perfectly in sync.

🔒

Zero Data Collection

No ads. No tracking. No accounts. No analytics SDKs. Your footage stays on your device. Period.

How It Works
Three Steps. Two Videos. One Take.

Open & Frame

Launch DualShot Recorder and frame your shot. Choose dual lens or single lens mode.

Hit Record

Tap the record button once. Both cameras capture simultaneously — portrait and landscape.

Post Everywhere

Two perfectly synced files save instantly to your Photos. Vertical for TikTok/Reels, horizontal for YouTube.
