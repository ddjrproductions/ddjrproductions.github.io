Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

DDJR Productions LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website ddjrproductions.com and the DualShot Recorder mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our websites or use our app.

1. DualShot Recorder App

DualShot Recorder is designed with privacy in mind. The app records video locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data, video content, or usage analytics from the app. All recordings remain entirely on your iPhone.

2. Website Analytics & Meta Pixel

Our website at ddjrproductions.com uses the Meta Pixel (formerly Facebook Pixel), a tracking technology provided by Meta Platforms, Inc. The Meta Pixel collects anonymous browsing data when you visit our site, including pages viewed, referring URLs, and general device information.

This data is used for:

  • Measuring the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns
  • Retargeting visitors with relevant ads on Facebook and Instagram
  • Understanding how visitors interact with our website

The Meta Pixel only loads after you provide consent via our cookie banner. If you decline, no tracking data is collected.

3. Cookies

Our website uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential cookies: Used to remember your cookie consent preference. These are necessary for the site to function properly.
  • Advertising cookies: Set by the Meta Pixel (with your consent) to enable ad targeting and measurement across Meta platforms.

You can manage or delete cookies at any time through your browser settings.

4. Your Choices & Opt-Out Rights

You have several options to control how your data is used:

  • Cookie consent: You can accept or decline non-essential cookies when you first visit our site. You can change your preference at any time by clearing your browser cookies and revisiting the site.
  • Meta ad settings: You can opt out of interest-based advertising from Meta by visiting Facebook Ad Preferences.
  • Industry opt-out: You can opt out of interest-based advertising from participating companies at optout.aboutads.info.

5. GDPR (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process your data based on your explicit consent. You have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal data. The Meta Pixel will not be activated unless you provide consent through our cookie banner.

6. CCPA (California Users)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information is collected about you, request deletion of your data, and opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information. To exercise your rights, contact us at the email below.

7. Third-Party Services

Our website may contain links to third-party websites such as the Apple App Store, Instagram, and Threads. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
