Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 15, 2026

DDJR Productions LLC ("we", "us", "our") built DualShot as a commercial application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use DualShot.

Information We Collect

DualShot does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. All video and audio recordings are saved locally to your device's Photos library and never leave your phone unless you choose to share them yourself.

Camera, Microphone, and Photos Access

DualShot requires access to your device's cameras, microphone, and Photos library solely to perform its core function: recording dual-lens video and saving the resulting files to your device. This data is processed entirely on your device and is never sent to any server or third party.

User Preferences

DualShot stores your settings (resolution, frame rate, file format, and camera assignment) locally on your device using Apple's standard UserDefaults storage. These preferences never leave your device.

Analytics and Tracking

DualShot does not use any analytics services, tracking frameworks, advertising SDKs, or third-party data collection tools. We do not track your usage or behavior in any way.

Third-Party Services

DualShot does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs that collect user data.

Children's Privacy

DualShot does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
