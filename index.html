About
From Durham to the World

Born and raised between Durham and Charlotte, North Carolina, Derrick Downey Jr. developed an early passion for performance and storytelling. What began as comedic and motivational posts on Instagram grew into a multi-platform career spanning content creation, acting, writing, and entrepreneurship.

In 2019, Oprah Winfrey featured one of his parody videos, catapulting him into mainstream recognition. He went on to win two Webby Awards for Best Short Form Video and Most Viral, and was invited to speak at USC's School of Dramatic Arts about the art of going viral and new media performance.

In 2021, he starred in the film Million Dollar Debunk, adding actor to an already stacked resume. Today, as the founder of DDJR Productions LLC, Derrick balances being a full-time content creator, children's author, app developer, and animal advocate — all while maintaining genuine connections with millions of followers who know him as the Squirrel Dad.

Maxine's Tale of Compassion

Inspired by a true story. When a tree was chopped down in Derrick's neighborhood, it turned out that a squirrel named Maxine had her nest and babies inside. Derrick rescued them, beginning a friendship that would inspire millions of followers and eventually become a children's book.

With beautiful illustrations and touching rhymes, Hand and Paw teaches kids the value of compassion, caring for animals, and appreciating the wonders of the natural world.

Available in hardcover, paperback, and audiobook — narrated by Derrick himself alongside Sydney Jay.
