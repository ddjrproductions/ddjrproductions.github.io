About

A creative brand built on heart, humor,
and human connection.

DDJR Productions LLC develops content, campaigns, and experiences that live at the intersection of wildlife, wellness, lifestyle, culture, and storytelling. The brand is anchored by Derrick's unique voice, signature humor, and ability to turn small moments into unforgettable narratives.

Whether partnering with global brands, speaking to the next generation of creatives, or expanding original intellectual property, DDJR Productions focuses on one core idea: connection that feels real.

Founder

Derrick Downey Jr.

Derrick Downey Jr. is a Webby Award-winning creator, storyteller, and entrepreneur best known for building a global community through his heartfelt and humorous wildlife content. With an audience of over 4 million across platforms, he has become a recognizable voice in digital storytelling, blending lifestyle, culture, and emotional connection into his work.

As the creative force behind DDJR Productions LLC, Derrick brings a founder-led perspective to every project — merging his on-camera presence, storytelling instincts, and audience trust with a production-driven approach. His work spans brand campaigns, original series, speaking engagements, and publishing, including his #1 bestselling children's book.

At the core of Derrick's work is a simple mission: to create stories that connect, uplift, and leave a lasting impact — whether through a single moment or a fully developed production.
